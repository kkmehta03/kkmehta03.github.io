What we do

We organize unique journeys throughout the world, visiting Pine trees on the way.

  • No mountain is too high for us, no desert too dusty, no snow storm too cold.
  • We camp next to the trees for days, sometimes weeks, observing every minute details of their exciting lives.
  • In return we gain insights into their long history, daily life and habits - and into our own lives.

Upcoming adventures

We have two special Pine journeys coming up this year:

Pine trees in urban environments

10 June - 21 August, South Europe

Pines are usually associated with high mountains and remote lakes. But did you know that Pine trees live in cities as well? Throughout this journey we’ll explore the coexistence of humans and Pines. Starting in Sicily, we’ll move up through Italy, trek through the Swiss mountains, camp at remote lakes in Slovenia and then descend through Croatian Adriatic coast. We’ll end the journey on the island of Hvar where we’ll hold our annual Pine tree celebration.

The sweet breeze of Pine trees

2 May - 15 July, Yunnan and Tibet

This journey is special - even according to our own standards! We’ll focus on the unique summer scent of Pine forests. We’ll start in Kunming and follow our noses to the Western mountain forests of Yunnan. From there we’ll climb up to the Tibetan plato where Pine trees flourish in one of the harshest climates in the world. Our main activities there: close our eyes, hear the breeze and smell the Pine trees.
